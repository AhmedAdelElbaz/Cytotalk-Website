Services

Research Consultation

CytoTalk Research Consultation Service provides you with experienced research experts to develop, and excute high-quality research focused on developmental biology, cancer and computational immunology. Our team would provide you with a list of available high-throughput datasets and discuss with you potential points to answer your research questions, generate hypotheses or apply for grants. To schedule your free consulation, contact us at info@cytotalk.com

Single Cell Data Analysis

Single cell analysis includes visualization and providing results to discuss available options and further analysis steps. Our team will analyze your in-house single cell datasets and any single cell dataset available on public domains. For more information, contact us at info@cytotalk.com

Mini-Courses

We provide mini-courses for individuals and research laboratories. Your lab members will acquire necessary skills to analyze high-throughput biological datasets. Our courses include differential expression analysis, gene set enrichment analysis, protein-protein interaction analysis, single cell analysis and others. For more information about our mini-courses, contact us at info@cytotalk.com
